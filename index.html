Welcome!!!

This site is designed as a tool for parents and teachers who wish to teach spanish to children in Kindergarten.

You, the parent/teacher, should read the instructions and questions for the child and guide them through the learning experience.

If you are working with a learner that already knows how to read instructions, feel free to let them operate independently, and you could serve as a monitor.

Please start by taking the pre-assessment with your learner. The Assessment is on the Get Started tab, then click on the "See what you know".

Then procced with the recommended level and follow the instructions in each lesson.

At the end of the final lesson you can take the final assessment. Have Fun!!!
